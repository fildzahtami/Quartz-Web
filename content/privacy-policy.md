## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026

Quartz is built with your privacy as a non-negotiable.

### What We Collect

Quartz does not collect, transmit, or sell any personally identifiable information. All data — including your focus sessions, doomscroll quota, Prisma balance, and app selections — is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's on-device frameworks.

### Screen Time Access

Quartz requests access to iOS Screen Time via Apple's Family Controls framework. This permission is used exclusively to apply app shields on your device. Quartz does not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted off-device.

### Third-Party Services

Quartz does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries.

### Changes to This Policy

If we ever update our privacy practices, we will post the updated policy here with a revised effective date.

### Contact

Questions? Concerns? We want to hear from you.

**Support:** Use TestFlight's built-in feedback feature, or contact us through the app.
